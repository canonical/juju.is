Charmhub: a marketplace for charms

Charmhub is a curated marketplace for charms which can be readily integrated into any infrastructure

Charmhub includes a large collection of Kubernetes and Machine charms, their related information and official documentation. All of the Canonical and community made charms are available on Charmhub.

You can find a curated selection of great charms below:

  • Databases
  • Big data and AI
  • Observability
  • Infrastructure

Join the community!

Matrix space

The Juju Matrix Space is the best way to interact with all the community members and chat with the Canonical engineers working on Juju and charming.

The Juju Matrix Space is hosted by Canonical and it is also the home of the broader Ubuntu community chat.

Discourse Forum

The discourse forum is the primary way for the community to discuss juju and charms. The forum is used daily by all the Canonical engineers working on charms and it includes a comprehensive list of community curated guides and resources.

The forum is the primary way for the development teams to share updates on the products, announce new releases and provide in depth explanations on new concepts.
